# Tutorial text: liquidtypes~~
Liquids behave somewhat weirdly in Minetest. Actually, there are 2 kinds of liquids.\nIf you watched the waterfall closely, you may have noticed that there is a slight difference\nbetween the water blocks that make the waterfall, and those up here in the basin.\n\nMinetest distinguishes between liquid source and flowing liquid.\n\nA liquid source block is always a full cube.\nA flowing liquid block looks slightly different. Often, it is not a full cube, but has a more or less\ntriangular shape. Also, flowing liquids usually have an unique "flowing" animation, but this may\nnot be the case for all liquids.\n\nUp in the basin, you see four rows of liquid sources, followed by one row of flowing\nliquids, followed by the waterfall itself.
